THE KRONOS LEGACY

The Essence of Time

Founded in Geneva, Switzerland, KRONOS has spent over a century engineering high-complication mechanical timepieces. Combining classical watchmaking principles with cutting-edge micro-mechanics, we create watches that are functional works of art designed to endure for generations.

1888

Swiss Geneva Origins

Elias Kronos establishes a small watchmaking atelier in Geneva, constructing custom chronometer escapements by hand for European nobility.

1912

The Chronograph Era

KRONOS patents its first mechanical pocket chronograph featuring split-second interval timing, setting a new standard for precision racing measurements.

1954

Deep Ocean Depths

Launching the Diver Titan prototype, featuring a hermetically sealed titanium case capable of withstanding pressures down to 300 meters deep.

2026

Caliber Masterpieces

Introducing skeletal titanium watch architecture, revealing the hand-polished movements and gears directly through the sapphire crystal dial.

ENGINEERING

Swiss Caliber Movements

Every KRONOS timepiece is powered by our in-house calibers. Composed of up to 300 micro-components, these mechanical engines are hand-assembled and adjusted in five positions to ensure chronometric perfection.

Using self-winding automatic rotors with 72-hour reserve capacities, the watches convert kinetic wrist movement into mechanical energy seamlessly.

MATERIALS

Grade 5 Titanium

To construct timepieces fit for the modern gentleman, we select resilient, biocompatible raw materials. Our cases are milled from solid blocks of aerospace-grade titanium and hand-polished to create contrasting brushed and specular facets.

Sealed under premium double-domed sapphire crystal with anti-reflective coating, the face provides scratch-proof visibility under any lighting conditions.
